About This Portal

This portal helps students find their belongings on campus. Whether you've lost something or found something, we're here to help connect items with their rightful owners.

Our community-driven platform makes it easy to report, search, and claim lost items. Together, we can keep our campus connected and help everyone find what they're looking for.
